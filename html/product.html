Initial survey

  • Our surveys will ask you a list of personalized questions regarding mental health
  • Depending on the answers you give, we will recommend different mental health exercises

Daily Exercises

  • Our app will recommend you exercises to be done every day
  • Exercises can be as short as 5 minutes, but the dedicated user can do longer activities
  • Our challenges are tailored to you, using a machine learning algorithm based on the mood and habits of the user

Additional Resources

  • We also provide a number of important mental health resources for people of all ages and skill levels
  • Our resources range from simple books for children to scholarly papers on mental health
  • You name it, we have it!

Point System

  • Through doing daily exercises, each user will be rewarded with points to spend in our shop!
  • Users can opt to buy premium rewards, such as removing ads from the app
  • Users can also spend their points on cosmetic badges or customizing their avatar
